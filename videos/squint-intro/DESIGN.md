# Design Specifications: Squint Video Composition

## Color Palette
*   **Background (Light)**: `#f9f9f9` (Off-white)
*   **Background (Accent)**: `#e2e2e2` (Light grey)
*   **Foreground/Primary**: `#000000` (Pitch Black)
*   **Accent (Primary)**: `#ff4d00` (Blaze Orange)
*   **Accent (Secondary)**: `#62ff96` (Neo Green)
*   **Card BG**: `#ffffff` (White)

## Typography
*   **Display Font**: Inter (weight: 800/900, black/uppercase display style)
*   **Body Font**: Inter (weight: 400, regular)
*   **Mono Labels**: JetBrains Mono (weight: 500/700, uppercase)

## Component Styles
*   **Neo-Brutalist Cards**:
    *   `border: 2px solid #000000;`
    *   `box-shadow: 8px 8px 0px 0px #000000;`
    *   `background-color: #ffffff;`
    *   `border-radius: 0px;` (Sharp corners)
*   **Neo-Brutalist Buttons**:
    *   `border: 2px solid #000000;`
    *   `box-shadow: 4px 4px 0px 0px #000000;`
    *   `background-color: #ff4d00;`
    *   `color: #ffffff;`
    *   `font-weight: 700;`
    *   `border-radius: 0px;`

## Do's & Don'ts
*   **DO** use bold borders, sharp corners, and high contrast.
*   **DO** stick to Inter and JetBrains Mono fonts.
*   **DO** utilize the Blaze Orange accent for major highlights and primary CTAs.
*   **DON'T** use smooth border-radii or gradient shadows.
*   **DON'T** use soft pastels (except off-white for backgrounds).
